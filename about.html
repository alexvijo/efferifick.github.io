About

Hello World

My name is Erick and I am a recent graduate from the University of Alberta with a BSc. in Computer Engineering. I am interested in technical topics like compilers, language, mathematics and signal processing; I also have a soft spot for new media art.

The name of this blog is a reference to Magritte's painting "The Treachery of Images". It is really obvious that a blog is not a person, but in today's world identity is blurred so much between the real and the virtual that sometimes people forget that one's not his/her social networking account. At least, I have done so in the past.
